About AB Enterprise

Established in 2010, AB Enterprise has grown into a leading provider of spare parts and industrial equipment. We are dedicated to maintaining an extensive inventory and delivering exceptional service to our valued customers.

Our commitment lies in understanding your unique requirements. We offer a comprehensive range of spare parts and equipment, ensuring your operations run smoothly and efficiently. We take pride in our ability to provide tailored solutions that meet the diverse needs of various industries.

Our Journey

  • 2010

    Our Humble Beginnings

    AB Enterprise was founded by two passionate engineers with a shared vision of creating innovative mechanical solutions.

  • 2008

    First Major Client

    We secured our first major industrial client, marking the beginning of our expansion into larger scale projects and establishing ourselves as a reliable engineering partner.

  • 2012

    New Headquarters

    With a growing team of 50 employees, we moved to our current headquarters, featuring state-of-the-art facilities, expanded manufacturing capabilities, and a dedicated R&D department.

  • 2015

    International Expansion

    We established our first international office in Singapore, opening doors to the Asian market and beginning our journey as a truly global company.

  • Today

    Continuing Our Mission

    With over 150 team members across three continents, we continue to push the boundaries of mechanical engineering while staying true to our founding principles of quality, innovation, and client satisfaction.
